About

Hello, 안녕하세요?

I’m Misun Kim from South Korea. If you don’t know how to pronounce my name, I always explain it mi as ‘me’ and the sun as ‘the sun’. The meaning of my name is not related to the sun or me though. I have a passion for web development and design. I studied computer engineering in graduate school. Also, I started my graphic design career as a volunteer in a rescue dog organization. The fields are quite similar and different but I found myself as a person who loves to create stuff in design and enjoy watching it have a function. I believe the quote's form follows function. It does not mean I make a low-quality visual stuff. I do love making stuff look good and visually improved. However, the functioning also means a lot to me. A fun fact for me, I have a Korean dental hygienist license and did my master's degree in image processing artificial intelligence. Those are not matching but I explored all the options and find the optimal choice for me. Those experiences give me a broad perspective. In my downtime, I usually enjoy reading books and drawing some illustrations. If you want to check my doodling, please check this Instagram. Also, I have fun to write essays in Korean in this link. If you want to work with me or chat with me, feel free to reach out to me. I described myself as an introvert but I love to listen to others’ stories. Thank you for your time to read this :)
